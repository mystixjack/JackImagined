Secure checkout

Complete your order.

Pay by card through Stripe or PayPal, or send an Interac e-Transfer if you're ordering from Canada. Every method is encrypted and none of your card details ever touch our servers.

Pay securely with Stripe

You'll be redirected to Stripe's secure checkout page to enter your card details. Stripe is PCI-compliant — your card number never passes through this site.

Powered by Stripe Payment Links. To combine multiple cart items into a single Stripe charge, connect a lightweight Checkout Session endpoint — see the project README, "Connecting Stripe."

Pay with PayPal

Log in with your PayPal account, or use a card via PayPal's guest checkout — no account required.

Buttons render live once you add your PayPal client ID in checkout.html — see README, "Connecting PayPal."

Pay with Interac e-Transfer

Available for Canadian customers. E-Transfers are sent manually through your bank, so orders are confirmed once payment is received rather than instantly.

How to pay:

  1. Send your e-Transfer to payments@jackimagined.com
  2. Use your order total as the amount, and set the security answer to sunrise
  3. Enter your order number in the transfer message
  4. Submit the confirmation form below

Orders are held for 48 hours awaiting payment, then confirmed by email once received — usually within one business day.

This form is a working demo — connect it to your inbox or a backend (e.g. Formspree, or a simple serverless function) to receive real submissions. See README, "Connecting Interac e-Transfer."
